## 🚀 Overview
Heic Converter for Windows is a free desktop utility that turns Apple's HEIC image files into the widely compatible JPG, JPEG, PNG, and PDF formats. It is built for users who receive HEIC photos from an iPhone or iPad and need to open, edit, share, or upload them on a Windows PC where HEIC is not natively supported everywhere. Drag in a single image or a whole folder, pick an output format, and the converter handles the rest with one click — no online upload required.

## ✨ Features
- **HEIC to JPG converter** — Convert HEIC images to high-quality JPG files in a single click, ready to open in any photo viewer, editor, or web app.
- **HEIC to PNG converter** — Export HEIC photos as PNG with lossless quality, ideal for editing, design work, or sharing on platforms that prefer PNG.
- **HEIC to PDF converter** — Turn one or several HEIC images into a single PDF document, perfect for sharing iPhone photos or sending them to print.
- **HEIC to JPEG converter** — Save HEIC photos as standard JPEG files with adjustable quality, so you keep the look you want at the file size you need.
- **Batch conversion** — Drop a whole folder of HEIC files at once and process hundreds of images in one pass with consistent output settings.
- **Free and offline** — A free HEIC to JPG converter for Windows that runs entirely on your PC, so your photos never leave your computer.
- **Metadata and quality control** — Choose to keep or strip EXIF data (date, location, camera info) and fine-tune output quality and resolution for each export.

## 🎯 Getting Started
1. Open Heic Converter and click **Add Files**, or drag a HEIC photo or folder directly into the app window.
2. Choose your target format — JPG, JPEG, PNG, or PDF.
3. Optionally adjust output quality, image resolution, or EXIF metadata settings.
4. Pick an output folder where the converted files should be saved.
5. Click **Convert** and open the output folder to find your converted images, ready to view, edit, or share.

## ❓ FAQ
**Is Heic Converter free?**
Yes. Heic Converter is a free HEIC to JPG converter for Windows, with no trial limits, no watermarks on the output files, and no required sign-up.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
Heic Converter runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit). Earlier Windows versions are not officially supported.

**Is my data secure?**
Yes. Heic Converter is an offline HEIC to JPEG converter — your photos are processed locally on your PC and are never uploaded to any server.

**Can it convert HEIC to PDF?**
Yes. The built-in HEIC to PDF converter mode can combine one or several HEIC images into a single PDF, or export each photo as its own PDF page.

**Does it support batch conversion?**
Yes. Drop an entire folder of HEIC files into the app and convert all of them to JPG, PNG, JPEG, or PDF in a single pass with the same settings.

**Do I need an internet connection to use it?**
No. Unlike a HEIC to JPG converter online, Heic Converter works fully offline once installed, so you can convert photos without uploading them anywhere.
